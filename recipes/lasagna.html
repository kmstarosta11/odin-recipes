Lasagana Recipe

Ingrediants

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat Ragu
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  1. Place layer of red sauce in bottom of tray
  2. Place layer of white sauce in tray
  3. Place cooked pasta on top of white sauce
  4. Add layer of cheese on top of pasta
  5. Repeat steps until tray is full
