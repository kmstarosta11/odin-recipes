Lasagana Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts, diced
  • 3 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery, diced
  • 1/2 white or yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp dried basil
  • 3 (32 oz) cans chicken stock
  • 1 chicken bouillon
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • Pinch salt
  1. Place chicken, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, basil, chicken stock and boullion into slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on high for 6 hours, or on low for 7-8 hours
  3. In a small bowl, whisk eggs until well beaten, then slowly use a fork to work in flour until a soft, non-sticky dough is formed
  4. Roll dough out very thin, use extra flour, if needed, to keep from sticking. For best results, roll between two pieces of parchment using a rolling pin
  5. emove top piece of parchment and use a knife or pizza cutter to cut dough into 1/4”x1” noodles
  6. Allow to airdry until soup is finished cooking.
